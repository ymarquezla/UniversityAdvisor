## Troubleshooting

### Issue: "Upload failed" or "Processing error"
**Solution**:
- Files may be too large (unlikely with markdown)
- Try uploading one document at a time
- Refresh the page and try again

### Issue: "NotebookLM can't find information"
**Solution**:
- Be more specific in your question
- Reference the document name directly
- Try rephrasing the question

### Issue: "Answers seem generic"
**Solution**:
- Ask more specific questions
- Reference specific universities or scholarships by name
- Use comparative questions ("Compare X vs Y")
