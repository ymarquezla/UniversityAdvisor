## INTERVIEW PREPARATION

### Technical Interview Prep
**Focus Areas**:
- Data Structures: Arrays, Lists, Hash Maps, Trees
- Algorithms: Sorting, Searching, Recursion
- Problem Solving: LeetCode Easy (50 problems), Medium (20 problems)

**Resources**:
- LeetCode (focus on Easy/Medium)
- HackerRank
- Cracking the Coding Interview (book)
- AlgoExpert (paid but excellent)

**Practice Schedule**:
- October-November: 30 minutes/day, Easy problems
- December-January: 1 hour/day, Easy + Medium problems
- February-March: 1-2 hours/day, Mock interviews

### Behavioral Interview Prep
**Common Questions**:
1. Tell me about yourself
2. Why do you want to work at [Company]?
3. Describe a challenging project you worked on
4. Tell me about a time you failed and what you learned
5. Why are you interested in tech/software engineering?
6. What are your strengths and weaknesses?
7. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
8. Tell me about a time you worked in a team

**STAR Method** (Situation, Task, Action, Result):
- Prepare 5-7 stories using STAR format
- Cover: teamwork, leadership, problem-solving, failure/learning, technical challenge
- Practice delivering concisely (2-3 minutes per story)

**Your Unique Stories to Emphasize**:
- **Trilingual Journey**: How learning 3 languages shaped your problem-solving
- **Python/REACT Projects**: Specific technical challenges you overcame
- **Passion for AI/Data Science**: Why this field excites you
- **Cultural Bridge**: How dual citizenship gives you unique perspective
- **Woman in STEM**: Challenges you've faced and how you've grown
